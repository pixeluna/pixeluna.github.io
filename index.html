Things I get asked a lot

Below you can find things I am asked fairly often and their answers. All the good basic stuff. :)

What liners do you use?

I use a mechanicl pencil to do the sketch. Then I use the Staedlers pigment liners (I have the 0.3 and 0.05 ones) to do the line art. Then I erase the sketch and done.

Did you go to art school?

Nope. For me it's all about self-teaching and practice.

What graphic tablet do you have?

Currently a Wacom Intuos S, but I also own a Wacom Intuos Pro M, but that one is in another city now. If you are a beginner the Intuos S is a perfect choice.

How long does it take you to make an illustration?

Hmmm, depends. Between 2 and 8 hours, usually.

What programme do you use to draw?

I use Adobe Illustrator and I love it. I think it's a perfect match for my line art style. Also I find the vector graphics incredibly useful.

Can you draw me for free?

No :)
