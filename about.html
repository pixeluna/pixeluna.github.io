About

Pixeluna started as a uni project of a Transylvanian student. That's me. I'm the Transylvanian student. Me that's speaking. Well, writing.
Anyways.
I have always been fascinated by visual art and I can vividly remember drawing on my grandma's walls and furniture when I was a toddler. The skill came with practice and a lot of patience and encouragement from my family. I was always "the kid that knew gow to draw" to the point where it became an extremely pregnant personality trait. Even though I never went to art school I still consider myself a tiny little artist. I study and evolve by trial and error. Of course I still have a lot to learn buy I believe that I am currently on the right path.
I started Pixeluna in November 2018. First came the Facebook page and the digital illustrations, then the Instagram profile and more drawings on paper. After I got the taste of it I tried to explore as many creative fields as I could.
Here are a few things I as Pixeluna have done so far: digital and on paper illustrations, handmade accessories, merch on Redbubble and an animaton (the one on this page, to be more exact).
For now I am looking forward to what the future has to bring, as I am sure we each get what we deserve :).
